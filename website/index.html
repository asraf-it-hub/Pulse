FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Pulse.

Is Pulse completely free and private?

Yes! Pulse is 100% free, private, and offline-first. Your local media library stays strictly on your device and is never uploaded anywhere.

How do I install the Android APK?

Download `pulse-android.apk` from this official website, tap the file in your downloads folder, and allow "Install from Unknown Sources" when prompted.

How do I run Pulse on Windows?

Download `pulse-windows.zip`, extract it to any folder on your PC, and double click `pulse.exe` to start playing.

What audio and video formats are supported?

MP3, FLAC, WAV, AAC, M4A, OGG, MP4, MKV, AVI, WebM, and TS streams with subtitle (`.srt`, `.vtt`) and multi-audio track support.
